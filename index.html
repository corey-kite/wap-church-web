About Our Church

MORE THAN A CHURCH

First Baptist Church Wapato, a congregation in Wapato, offers members — and visitors — a chance to become part of something greater: to grow in their love of God and for one another within our beautiful community.

We are rooted in the stories of the Bible and believe worship is something that can be expressed in everything we do. Our philosophy is founded on the life and teachings of Jesus Christ. No matter who you are, there’s a special place for you at our church. Get in touch today or come and visit us in person.

Mission Statement

We exist to exalt Jesus Christ as Lord by seeking, teaching and caring for the people in our Church, Community and the World.

“This church is the pillar and stable foundation of truth, because in it soundeth the voice of the Son of God” ~John Philpot

Meet the Pastor

Pastor Richard Burson serves the congregation of First Baptist Church Wapato with a heart for the Yakima Valley and a love for preaching God’s Word.

He is committed to teaching the Bible clearly, caring for the church family, and equipping believers to live out their faith in everyday life.
