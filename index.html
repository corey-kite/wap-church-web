About Our Church

First Baptist Church Wapato is a Bible-believing congregation committed to worship, discipleship, and service. We gather to study God’s Word, encourage one another, and reach out to our neighbors in the name of Jesus.

Whether you’ve been walking with Christ for many years or you’re just beginning to ask questions about faith, you are welcome here.

Meet the Pastor

Pastor Richard Burson serves as the pastor of First Baptist Church Wapato. He has a heart for the Yakima Valley, a love for preaching God’s Word, and a desire to see people grow in a real, living relationship with Jesus Christ.

Pastor Richard is available for prayer, spiritual counsel, and encouragement. If you’d like to connect, you can reach the church office at (509) 877-6373 or email richard.burson@outlook.com.
